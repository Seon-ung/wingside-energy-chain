Smart Contract Architecture

EnergyToken.sol
ERC-3643 compliant security token representing fractional ownership of EV charging station revenue. Includes transfer restrictions and compliance hooks.
EnergyDEX.sol
On-chain order book for P2P trading of energy tokens. Supports limit orders with USDC pairs and automatic compliance verification.
RevenueVault.sol
Collects charging station revenue and distributes proportional yields to token holders. Automated claim mechanism with compound option.
EnergyOracle.sol
Chainlink-compatible oracle contract receiving off-chain station data feeds ‚Äî kWh dispensed, revenue, uptime, and session counts.
IdentityRegistry.sol
On-chain KYC registry managing investor identities and eligibility claims. Gates all STO transfers through compliance checks.
ComplianceModule.sol
Modular compliance rules engine ‚Äî investor count limits, transfer restrictions, jurisdiction checks, and holding period enforcement.
